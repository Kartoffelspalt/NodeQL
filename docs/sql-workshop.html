NodeQL SQL Cheat-Sheet

SELECTSELECT name, city chooses the visible columns.
FROMFROM customers identifies the table.
WHEREWHERE city = 'Berlin' filters individual rows.
JOININNER JOIN orders ON customers.id = orders.customer_id connects tables through keys.
GROUP BYGROUP BY city creates groups for COUNT, SUM, or AVG.
ORDER BYORDER BY total DESC sorts the result.
